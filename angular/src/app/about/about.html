About(Apple)

Founded in 1976 by Steve Jobs, Steve Wozniak, and Ronald Wayne, it is known for products like the iPhone, iPad, and Mac computers, as well as its integrated hardware and software design. Headquartered in Cupertino, California, Apple is a major global company, and its business operations span the Americas, Europe, the Middle East, Africa, and Asia-Pacific.

History and growth

* Founded on April 1, 1976, initially as Apple Computer Company.
* Incorporated in 1977 as Apple Computer, Inc.
* The company's name was changed to Apple Inc. in 2007 to reflect its expansion beyond computers into consumer electronics.
* It rose to become a market leader by producing well-designed, user-friendly versions of products like personal computers, iphones & tablets.
* After the passing of Steve Jobs in 2011, Tim Cook became the CEO & the company has continued to thrive, launching products such as the
  Apple Watch.
